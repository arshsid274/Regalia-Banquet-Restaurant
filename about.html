Our History

Regalia Banquet & Resort in Gorakhpur offers elegant venues, luxurious rooms, and exceptional service, ideal for weddings, events, and comfortable stays.

Regalia Banquet & Resort in Gorakhpur is a premier destination for weddings, events, and luxurious stays. The property features elegant banquet halls, well-appointed rooms, and a variety of amenities including a swimming pool, restaurant, and kids' play area. Guests appreciate its convenient location near Gorakhnath Temple and Nakaha Jungle Railway Station, as well as the attentive staff and quality service. With a 4.6/5 rating, it's a top choice for celebrations and comfortable accommodations.
